How can we help you?

We hope you have found an answer to your question. If you need any help, please search your query on our Support Center or contact us via email.

Our suppliers typically ship items within one to five days of receiving an order. From that point, it takes between 20 up to 30 days for items to arrive. As soon as your product ships, we'll send you an email notification to let you know it's on its way. You'll then receive a tracking number that will let you keep tabs of where your order is at all times.

Instead of returning items, you can contact us for a full refund. Why? Returns run counter to our emphasis on sustainability: every return has a carbon footprint. So just email us and tell us what went wrong, send along a pic, and well give you your money back in full. Then, if possible, you can donate your product to a local charity or recycle it.

Nail Art Bay source a range of products from various suppliers for you to achieve the best nail art designs. Consequently, some items will be shipped separately and should arrive in a similar timeframe.
